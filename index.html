Maroon 5

Introduction

Maroon 5 started making music in 2002.

Members of Maroon 5

A picture of Maroon 5: There are eight members in the band, though originally there were five. Hence, the name Maroon 5.
  • Adam Levine--Main and only singer in Maroon 5
  • James Valentine--First guitarist in Maroon 5
  • Mickey Madden--Second guitarist in Maroon 5
  • Sam Farrar--Third guitarist in Maroon 5
  • Matt Flynn--First drummer in Maroon 5
  • Ryan Dusick--Second drummer in Maroon 5
  • Jesse Carmichael--Pianist for Maroon 5
  • PJ Morton--Plays keyboard for Maroon 5
Album Title Album Cover Album Description
Singles Singles contains twelve Maroon 5 original songs and collaborations. It was published in 2015, and contains famous songs such as "Payphone", and "Moves Like Jagger".
Helix:Volume 1 "What Lovers Do" on the album Helix:Volume 1 was published in 2017. This album, similar to Helix:Volume 2 was made up of twenty different songs from twenty different bands and artists. Therefore, in the two volumes of Helix, Maroon 5 has published their most recent and currently most popular songs "Wait" and "What Lovers Do"
Red Pill Blues Red Pill Blues was an album with entirely Maroon 5 music, along with some songs that collaborate with other artists. It was published in 2017, and has fifteen songs. It contains hit songs from Maroon 5, such as "Girls Like You", "Cold", "Don't Wanna Know", as well as "Wait".
V V was an album with entirely Maroon 5 songs and collaborations as well. It has fifteen songs, and was published in 2014. It contains Maroon 5 hit songs such as the infamous "Sugar", and "Animals".
Helix:Volume 2 "Wait" on the album Helix:Volume 2 was Maroon 5's most recent contribution, as it was published in 2018. However, this album was not solely made by Maroon 5. It was actually a compilation of twenty songs from twenty different bands or singers. Maroon 5's newest hit "Wait" is featured on Helix:Volume 2.
