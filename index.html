  • Sam Farrar--Third guitarist in Maroon 5
  • Matt Flynn--First drummer in Maroon 5
  • Ryan Dusick--Second drummer in Maroon 5
  • Jesse Carmichael--Pianist for Maroon 5
  • PJ Morton--Plays keyboard for Maroon 5
